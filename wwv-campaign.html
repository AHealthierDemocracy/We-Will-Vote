The Individual Pledge

"I know that the health of my community hinges on the health of our democracy and that robust civic participation is required to advance health equity for every community. I join with my colleagues and institutions across the health sector to assert: We Will Vote."

  • I will vote in upcoming elections
  • I will work with my professional organizations and/or workplace to promote civic health
  • I will encourage friends, family, and colleagues to vote
  • I will support elections through volunteer roles in my community

Three Ways to Get Involved
  1. 1

    Take the Individual Pledge

    Commit to vote and to promote voter participation in your workplace and community. The pledge is quick, nonpartisan, and amplified by every colleague who joins you.

  2. 2

    Sign Your Organization On

    Make an organizational commitment to We Will Vote. Health systems, professional associations, FQHCs, and community health organizations are all welcome to sign on.

  3. 3

    Share Your Story

    Record a 30-second video about why voting matters to you and your patients. Stories from health workers are among the most powerful voices in civic life.

About Vot-ER

Vot-ER is bridging a gap in care with a groundbreaking prescription: civic engagement. Our mission is to empower patients and healthcare professionals to address the root causes of health disparities through civic participation. Our HIPAA-compliant resources promote employee happiness, combat burnout, and cultivate deeper trust between healthcare professionals and patients.

Since 2020, we've expanded our nonpartisan work to 1,700+ participating sites reaching 80,000+ individuals — with an 80% voter turnout rate nationally in 2024. We Will Vote is our most ambitious coordinated action yet: mobilizing the entire health sector as a force for civic health. Featured by The New York Times, CNN, Washington Post, NBC, NPR, and PBS NewsHour.
