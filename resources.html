Civic Calendar

Civic holidays to activate

Use these national civic holidays as hooks for your internal communications, social media posts, and voter mobilization activities. We Will Vote's Evergreen Comms Kit includes graphics for each.

August
Civic Health Month

A full month to promote civic engagement in your organization — use the field guide and comms kit to plan activities, messaging, and pledge drives.

September 17
National Voter Registration Day

The single biggest voter registration day of the year. Activate your Vot-ER badges, post to social media, and send a registration reminder to your staff and members.

October 7–11
National Voter Education Week

Five days to educate your community about the ballot — ballot measures, local races, how to vote, and why it matters for health.

October 29
National Vote Early Day

Encourage your staff, members, and patients to vote early. Share early voting locations and hours, and promote the make-a-plan tool.

November
Election Day

The culmination of the campaign. Encourage flexible scheduling, share last-minute voting information, and celebrate your organization's civic health champions.
